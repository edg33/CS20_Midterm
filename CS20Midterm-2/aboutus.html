About Us

Our Story:
Nestled in the heart of our city, Zesty's was born from a dream to share the wonders of Italian cuisine with our community. Our founders, Mario and Lucia, both hailing from the picturesque regions of Tuscany and Sicily, brought with them a deep love for the culinary traditions of their homeland. In 2010, Zesty's opened its doors, inviting guests to savor the authentic flavors of Italy in a cozy and elegant setting. Our menu, inspired by the diverse regions of Italy, features a range of dishes from hearty pastas to exquisite seafood, all prepared with the freshest ingredients and a touch of creativity. Join us at Zesty's, where every meal is a journey to the heart of Italy.

Our Mission:
At Zesty's, our mission is to provide an authentic Italian dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary traditions of Italy. We are committed to using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients to create dishes that transport our guests to the heart of Italy with every bite. Our passion for Italian cuisine is matched only by our dedication to warm, attentive service, ensuring that every visit to Zesty's is a memorable one. We strive to create a welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy the timeless flavors of Italy, and to share in the joy of good food and good company.
